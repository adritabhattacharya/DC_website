Get into Character

We Love Theatre

With a motto of "Get into Character", VIT Dramatics Club is
a cultural club in VIT, Vellore, Tamil Nadu that focuses on
enhancing various art forms of theatre.

Our main domains are Stage Plays, Street Plays and Mime.

Our Plays

TT Amphitheatre is our home and this is where the magic happens.

Happily Ever After

The stories we were told as children, definitely, mark us for life. At one point or the other, all of us have come across stories where two people meet, fall in love, get married, and live happily ever after. But, is love actually a fairytale story? Join us, as we bring to you the story of Madhav and Tara, the romantic story where we always have happy endings and perfect love... or do we?

Virudaavali

Have you ever wondered, what exactly does it take to stand at the borders, protecting the nation, with the responsibility of more than a billion people on your shoulders? Why not take a sneak peek! 'Virudavali', the winner of Khalbali at Riviera'20, is out on YouTube.

The End?

Children are blessings of God, aren't they? They have their own desires and dreams. Their loved ones also have ambitions for them. But what if they are not the same? Here's the team of "The End?", the winner of this Riviera's Mime Competition Kintraclash. "The End?", is a tale not far from reality showing what happens when children are forced to pursue things they don't like and are used as mere pawns to compete against each other?

Claustrophobia

‘It’s just a first date, what could possibly go wrong?’ We don’t run into people by accident. They are meant to cross our path for a reason. Meet Anjali Naidu, a short-tempered, perpetually-annoyed cardiologist. And meet Varun Malhotra, the brownie enthusiast with a devil-may-care attitude... What happens when they are set up for an arranged marriage? Oh do the sparks fly...
